Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I upload my products?

On the navigation bar you see on the right click the Upload link.

Then click on View retail sample and upload a csv file in that format with all the non price information about the products.

How Do I upload my labor items?

On the navigation bar you see on the right click the Upload link.

Then click on View labor sample and upload a csv file in that format with all the labor item information about the products.

How Do I upload my material items?

On the navigation bar you see on the right click the Upload link.

Then click on View material sample and upload a csv file in that format with all the material item information about the products.

How Do I upload my price information?

On the navigation bar you see on the right click the Upload link.

Then click on View price sample and upload a csv file in that format with all the price information about the products.

How do I update and add categories, subcategories,products, or groups?

On the navigation bar you see on the right click the Home Link.

For respective category click on the edit link and to add click on the add link for the respective category type(Category, Subcategory, Group, Product)

How do I update and add product variations?

Find the product by going home and selecting it's category, subcategory and group. Then click on edit for the corresponding product or product variation.
